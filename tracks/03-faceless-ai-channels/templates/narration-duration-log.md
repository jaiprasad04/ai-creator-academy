### Editing Guidelines:
* **The Audio Spine Rule:** Never generate video clips first and stretch audio to fit. Lay down the narration, trim the pauses, and crop the video to the audio boundaries.
* **Cut Pacing:** A visual transition should occur every **2 to 4 seconds** in short-form video to prevent drop-off.
