# Production Schedule Tracker

Use this tracker to schedule and coordinate batched content runs across multiple channels.

## Weekly Batching Schedule

| Day | Task | Status / Goal |
|---|---|---|
| **Monday** | **Research & Scripting** | Draft and outline 5 scripts using the retention template. |
| **Tuesday** | **Audio Generation** | Batch-generate voice narration files; trim pauses. |
| **Wednesday**| **Asset Harvesting & Video Gen** | Generate storyboard elements and render video background clips. |
| **Thursday** | **Timeline Editing** | Assemble timelines, add captions, color grade, and master audio. |
| **Friday** | **Scheduling & Upload** | Write descriptions, tags, design thumbnails, and upload queued posts. |
