# AI Corporate Headshot Style & Lighting Guide

Use this reference guide to configure studio lighting setups, executive wardrobe options, and prompt parameters for consistent corporate portrait generation.

---

## 📸 Lighting Setup Styles

### 1. Corporate Rembrandt (Executive Default)
* **Description:** Classic key light positioned at 45 degrees, creating a soft triangle of light on the shadow cheek. Professional, commanding, and polished.
* **Prompt Anchor:**
  > `"Studio portrait photograph, soft Rembrandt key light, subtle shadow side fill, soft backlight rim separating shoulders, neutral dark grey gradient studio background, 85mm portrait lens, f/2.8 depth of field."`

### 2. Modern Tech / Soft Butterfly
* **Description:** Key light placed high and directly in front, creating a soft shadow under the nose. Friendly, warm, and approachable. Ideal for startups and tech founders.
* **Prompt Anchor:**
  > `"Studio portrait photograph, high softbox butterfly lighting, soft warm fill light, friendly approachable smile, bright modern glass office background with soft bokeh, 85mm lens, photorealistic skin texture."`

### 3. Creative Editorial / High Key
* **Description:** Bright, evenly lit studio background with high key lighting. Energetic and clean. Perfect for creative agencies, consultants, and keynote speakers.
* **Prompt Anchor:**
  > `"Editorial studio portrait, bright high key lighting, crisp white studio background, vibrant soft lighting, sharp focus on eyes, clean modern aesthetic, 85mm lens."`

## 👔 Executive Wardrobe Descriptors

Select wardrobe tokens matching the company's dress code:

* **Formal Executive:** `"tailored charcoal navy suit blazer, crisp white button-down dress shirt, silk tie, professional corporate style"`
* **Business Casual:** `"dark navy blazer, open-collar light blue dress shirt, no tie, modern executive look"`
* **Tech Founder:** `"sleek dark grey fitted turtleneck sweater, minimalist stylish aesthetic"`
* **Creative Professional:** `"structured black blazer, plain white crewneck t-shirt, modern agency style"`

## 🚫 Negative Prompt Library

Always append this negative prompt string to enforce realistic skin textures and prevent artificial AI artifacts:

```
plastic skin, mannequin face, over-smoothed skin, extra teeth, asymmetric eyes, cartoon, 3d render look, oversaturated, harsh direct flash, warped neck, deformed ears, low resolution, blurry, bad anatomy, double chin distortion, glossy forehead
```
