# UGC Script Template

**Product:** ___________
**Target length:** 25-30 seconds (~75-90 words)

## Hook (0-2 sec)
[A question, bold claim, or visual surprise that stops the scroll]

## Problem / Pitch (2-15 sec)
[What problem does the viewer have, and how does this product solve it — said like you'd tell a friend, not like ad copy]

## Proof / Demo (15-25 sec)
[Show it working, a result, or a specific detail that makes it credible]

## Call to Action (25-30 sec)
[One clear next step — "link in bio," "use code X," "swipe up"]

---

### Checklist before recording
- [ ] Hook works with sound off (captions alone should make sense)
- [ ] No more than one CTA
- [ ] Sounds like speech, not a script, when read aloud
